## Ad Creative Guidelines

**Visual Style:**
- Clean, minimalist design
- NO personal photos or faces
- Use ceremony details, textures, or abstract patterns
- Professional color palette (sage green, cream, gold accents)

**Brand Voice:**
- Factual and informative
- No emotional language
- No "we/I/our journey" language
- Professional distance maintained

**CTA Language (Use consistently):**
- "Start With Clarity"
- "Request Planning Overview"
- "Access Budget Calculator"
- Always include subtext: "No obligation · Complete confidentiality"

---

## Budget Allocation Recommendations

- Start with $500-1000/month testing budget
- Allocate 60% to privacy/budget messaging angles
- 30% to cultural expertise/sourcing angles
- 10% to breaking tradition/fusion angles
- Test audience segments separately

**Key Metrics:**
- Focus on Cost Per Lead (CPL) under $15
- Landing page conversion rate >5%
- Consultation booking rate from leads >20%
